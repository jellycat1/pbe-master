How to Support

PBE Master is a free study tool created to help young people and their clubs dive deeper into God’s Word while preparing for the Pathfinder Bible Experience together.

Your support keeps PBE Master free of ads and fees, covering essential costs like Bible licensing, hosting, and ongoing development. PBE Master is developed through VineTech, a nonprofit with 501(c)3 status pending—your gift may become tax-deductible once approved. Join us in empowering Pathfinders everywhere by donating today.
