How to Support

PBE Master was created by a Pathfinder for Pathfinders—a free study tool designed to help young people and their clubs prepare for the Pathfinder Bible Experience together. This new resource equips Pathfinders with focused study support and encourages deeper engagement with God’s Word.

Your support can help this mission grow. Donations from parents, clubs, and churches make it possible to cover essential costs like Bible licensing, hosting, and continued development—keeping PBE Master free of ads and fees for every Pathfinder.

PBE Master operates under VineTech, a nonprofit organization with 501(c)(3) status pending. Gifts may become tax-deductible once approved. Join us in empowering the next generation of Pathfinders—make a gift today to help keep PBE Master freely available for all.
