How to Support

PBE Master was created by a passionate Pathfinder who combined his love for the Pathfinder Bible Experience and computer science to help others study God’s Word more effectively. After participating in his first year of PBE, he was inspired to make a tool that would make Scripture study and quiz practice more engaging and accessible for everyone. During the summer of 2025, he dedicated his time to developing this app—turning his firsthand experience as a Pathfinder into a resource for others who share the same mission of learning the Bible deeply.

This project became a true team effort. His family and his PBE teammate, Joshua, worked alongside him to carefully review and refine hundreds of quiz questions to ensure accuracy and clarity. What began as a summer coding project has grown into a community resource built by Pathfinders, for Pathfinders—designed to help anyone prepare with confidence, grow in knowledge, and strengthen their connection with Scripture.
